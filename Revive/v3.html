FAQ

Common questions.

What exactly is the free demo redesign?

It's a strategic redesign direction for your homepage — covering layout, messaging, UX, and visual direction. Think of it as a preview of what your site should become, delivered within 48 hours of your submission.

Is there any obligation?

None at all. The demo redesign is genuinely free. If you like what you see and want to move forward, we'll scope a full project together. If not, you keep the insights.

How long does the full redesign take?

Typically 3–6 weeks depending on scope. We work efficiently without cutting corners — most single-page redesigns launch within 3 weeks.

Do you only design, or also build?

Both. We handle design and full-stack development. Whether it's a Shopify theme, Framer site, Webflow build, or custom codebase — we deliver the finished product, not just a Figma file.

Can you work with Shopify, Framer, Webflow, or custom sites?

Yes — all of them. We're platform-agnostic. We'll recommend the best stack based on your goals, or work within your existing setup if that's preferred.

What kind of businesses do you work with?

Founders, e-commerce brands, hospitality businesses, premium service companies, and lifestyle brands. If you sell something online and your website doesn't match the quality of your product, we're a good fit.

How is Revive different from other agencies?

We combine premium design with AI-enhanced analysis and conversion-first thinking. We don't build pretty pages — we build pages that perform. Every decision is informed by data and user behaviour, not just aesthetics.
