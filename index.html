Three functions.
Running simultaneously.
Every second.

Most pool systems do one thing at a time. KontrolWater runs three core processes continuously — sensing, dosing, and predicting — without pause, without manual input, without relying on a scheduled visit.

01
Continuous Sensing

Twelve embedded probes measure pH, ORP, temperature, conductivity, flow rate, and filter pressure — taking a complete reading every 10 seconds. Manual testing happens once a day at best. By the time a problem is detected, it's already a problem. KontrolWater never stops checking.

02
Intelligent Dosing

Based on live sensor data, KontrolWater calculates the exact chemical correction required and delivers it via peristaltic pumps — accurate to ±0.01 pH. Over-dosing wastes spend and irritates swimmers. Under-dosing creates compliance risk. KontrolWater doses precisely what's needed, when it's needed.

03
Predictive Alerts

Pattern recognition tracks degradation across sensors, dosing frequency, and pressure differentials. A blocked filter, a failing pump, or a chemical imbalance each give signals days before they become critical. KontrolWater reads those signals. You receive a notification instead of an emergency.
