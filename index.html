About KontrolWater

Traditional pool systems require constant manual attention — testing, adjusting chemicals, cleaning filters, monitoring pumps. KontrolWater replaces all of that with a single, self-contained intelligent unit.

Installed directly into your existing plumbing, KontrolWater continuously reads water chemistry, flow rate, temperature, and filter pressure. It responds in real time — no schedules, no guesswork, no service calls.

Three core functions operating simultaneously, every second of every day.

01
Continuous Sensing

Embedded probes measure pH, ORP, temperature, conductivity, and flow at 10-second intervals. No manual testing. No estimation.

02
Intelligent Dosing

KontrolWater calculates the precise chemical correction required and doses automatically via peristaltic pumps — accurate to within 0.01 pH.

03
Predictive Alerts

Machine learning models identify degradation patterns before they manifest. Maintenance teams receive alerts before there is a problem to solve.
