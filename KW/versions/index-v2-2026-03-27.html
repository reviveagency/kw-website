Three processes.
Simultaneously.
Always.

Traditional systems wait for the next scheduled check. KontrolWater reads, calculates, and acts in real time — continuously, without human input.

01
Continuous Sensing

12 probes measure pH, ORP, temperature, conductivity, flow, and pressure every 10 seconds. Manual testing happens once a day at best — by the time a problem shows up, it's already a problem. KontrolWater never stops checking.

02
Precise Dosing

KontrolWater calculates the exact correction needed and delivers it via peristaltic pumps — accurate to ±0.01 pH. Not approximately right. Exact. Over-dosing wastes money. Under-dosing creates risk. This does neither.

03
Predictive Alerts

Pattern recognition identifies degradation before it becomes a failure. A blocked filter, a failing pump, or a pressure drop each signal days in advance. You get a notification. Not an emergency.

Engineered in Europe

Built by engineers who refused to accept scheduled guesswork.

Timer-based dosing systems don't know what's in the water. They add chemicals on a schedule — regardless of actual conditions. The result: over-dosing, under-dosing, and dependence on service visits to catch the difference. That's not pool management. It's reactive maintenance dressed up as a system.

KontrolWater was built to replace it. Our engineers come from industrial water treatment, sensor hardware, and building management systems. Every unit is manufactured and tested at our European facility. Every installation is backed by a 5-year warranty and the same technical team that built the system.
